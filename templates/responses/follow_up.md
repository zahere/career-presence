# Follow-up Email Templates

## 7-Day Follow-up (No Response)

**Subject:** Following up on {role} application - {your_name}

Hi {recruiter_name},

I hope this message finds you well. I wanted to follow up on my application for the {role} position at {company} that I submitted on {date}.

I remain very enthusiastic about the opportunity to contribute to {company}'s work in {relevant_area}. My experience with {relevant_experience} aligns well with what you're looking for.

I'd welcome the chance to discuss how my background could benefit your team. Please let me know if you need any additional information from me.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Best regards,
{your_name}

---

## Accepting an Offer

**Subject:** Accepting {role} offer - {your_name}

Dear {hiring_manager},

I am thrilled to formally accept the offer for the {role} position at {company}.

Thank you for this opportunity. I'm excited to join the team and contribute to {company_mission_or_project}.

As discussed, I understand my start date will be {date} and the compensation will be {details as discussed}.

Please let me know if there's any paperwork or onboarding steps I should complete before my start date.

I look forward to joining the team!

Best regards,
{your_name}

## Declining an Offer (Gracefully)

**Subject:** Re: {role} offer

Dear {hiring_manager},

Thank you so much for extending the offer for the {role} position at {company}. I truly appreciate the time you and the team invested in the interview process.

After careful consideration, I have decided to pursue a different opportunity that more closely aligns with my current career goals. This was not an easy decision, as I was impressed by {specific_positive_aspect}.

I hope our paths cross again in the future, and I wish you and the team continued success.

Thank you again for your consideration.

Best regards,
{your_name}
